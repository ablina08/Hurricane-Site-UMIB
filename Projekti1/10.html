Hurricane Safety Tips

Prepare for a Hurricane

  • Have an emergency kit ready with food, water, and first aid supplies.
  • Know your evacuation routes and shelters.
  • Stay informed with weather updates and alerts.
  • Secure outdoor items that can become projectiles in high winds.
  • Charge your devices and have backup power sources.

During a Hurricane

  • Stay indoors and away from windows.
  • Follow instructions from local authorities.
  • Monitor emergency broadcasts for updates.
  • Avoid using candles to reduce fire risk.

After a Hurricane

  • Wait for authorities to declare it safe before going outside.
  • Avoid downed power lines and flooded areas.
  • Document damage for insurance claims.
  • Help neighbors and check on vulnerable individuals.
